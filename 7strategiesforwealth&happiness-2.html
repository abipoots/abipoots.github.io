Unleash the Power of Goals

  1. Write Down Your Goals
    1. What do I want to do?
    2. What do I want to be?
      3. e.g.in 10 years, 5 years, 3 years, 1 year
    3. What do I want to see?
    4. What do I want to have?write a time goal for each
    5. Where do I want to go?
    6. What would I like to share?
  2. Choose the 4 most important from each time period (10,5,3,1)
    For each goal, write down:
  3. A detailed description of what you want.
  4. The reason WHY you want to achieve/acquire it.
    (Note: if you can't come up with one, it's a whim, not a true goal, and should be replaced)
    COPY INTO A PERMANENT JOURNAL AND REVIEW ONCE A WEEK.
    CARRY WITH THEM AT YOU ALL TIMES.
  5. Write down what you will experience when you reach your goals.
  6. Make a list of resources you have to help you achieve your goals.
  7. Think of the times in your life when you were most successful.
  8. Write down the type of person you would have to be to attain your goals.
  9. Write down why you haven't already achieved these goals (what prevented you before)
  10. Create a step-by-step plan to achieve your goals.

    11. Seek Knowledge

    • Capture Special Moments
    • Capture Ideas
    • Capture Knowledge

      • Reflect (personal reflection)

      Learn from others (Books, podcasts etc.)

      LISTEN (e.g. take a successful person out to dinner)

      OBSERVE

      Invest time and money into a consistent, disciplined, purposeful search for knowledge.

      Learn How to Change

      Learn to work harder on yourself than on your job.


      To have more than what you've got, become more than you are.


      Get excited about discipline - it is the only vehicle for real progress.


      Control Your Finances

      Put together your financial statement. (Assets|Liabilities)

      Take control of your income and expenditure.

      Pay your taxes with a positive attitude.

      Master Time

      Don't start your day until you have it finished.
      Don't start your week until you have it finished.
      Don't start your month until you have it finished.

      Surround Yourself with Winners

      Analyse who you spend time with, and the impact/influence of that. Think:

      • What do you do?
      • What do you listen to?
      • What do you read?
      • Where do you go?
      • What do you think?
      • What do you talk about?
      • How do you feel with them?

      Learn the Art of Living Well

      Be happy with what you have while pursuing what you want.
      Save up for special things.
      "The good life" is an attitude, an act, an idea, a discovery, a search; it's a lifestyle that provides you with a constant joy in living.

      Final Points

      There are four basic emotions which cause people to act:

      1. DISGUST
        i.e. hitting rock bottom; "I've had enough"
      2. DECISION
        Decide! (don't camp at the fork in the road)
      3. DESIRE
        Comes from the inside but may be triggered by outside forces.
        Search for your "hot button".
      4. RESOLVE

        I WILL


        Promise yourself you'll never give up.

      Why should you try?
      Why not?
      Why not you?
      Why not you now?

      ASK FOR GOD'S HELP.
